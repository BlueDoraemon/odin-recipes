Time Lord Tea

Are you a fan of Doctor Who and looking for a way to channel your inner Time Lord? Look no further than Time Lord Tea! This tea recipe is inspired by the Eleventh Doctor's love for a good cuppa and is perfect for any Doctor Who-themed party or just a cozy night in.

Ingredients

  • 1 black tea bag
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground ginger
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cardamom
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground cloves
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1/4 cup milk (dairy or non-dairy)

Method

  1. Bring a pot of water to a boil and steep a black tea bag for 3-5 minutes, or until the tea is brewed to your desired strength.
  2. In a small bowl, mix together the cinnamon, ginger, cardamom, cloves, and nutmeg.
  3. Add the spice mixture to the brewed tea and stir in the honey.
  4. In a separate small saucepan, heat up the milk until it is hot but not boiling.
  5. Froth the milk using a frother or whisk until it becomes creamy and bubbly.
  6. Pour the spiced tea into a mug and top it off with the frothed milk.

And just like that, you've got a steaming cup of Time Lord Tea! Sip on this cozy and flavorful tea while you watch your favorite Doctor Who episodes or use it as a delicious addition to your Doctor Who-themed party. Enjoy!
