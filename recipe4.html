Jammie Dodgers

Jammie Dodgers are a classic British treat that have made several appearances on the Doctor Who series, especially during the Eleventh Doctor's tenure. These biscuits are a perfect combination of shortbread and jam, and they're super easy to make at home. So, put on your apron and get ready to channel your inner Time Lord with this simple Jammie Dodgers recipe.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup raspberry jam

Method

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a large mixing bowl, cream together the softened butter and sugar until it's light and fluffy.
  3. Gradually add in the flour, mixing until a soft dough forms.
  4. Turn the dough out onto a floured surface and roll it out to 1/4 inch thickness.
  5. Use a circular cookie cutter to cut out biscuits from the dough. Then, using a smaller circular cutter, cut out the centers of half of the biscuits.
  6. Place the biscuits onto a baking sheet lined with parchment paper, and bake them for 12-15 minutes, or until the edges are lightly golden brown.
  7. Remove the biscuits from the oven and allow them to cool for a few minutes on the baking sheet before transferring them to a wire rack to cool completely.
  8. Once the biscuits have cooled, spread a spoonful of raspberry jam onto the whole biscuits.
  9. Top each jam-covered biscuit with a biscuit with a hole in the center to create the classic Jammie Dodgers sandwich.

Your homemade Jammie Dodgers are now ready to enjoy! Serve them with a cup of tea for the ultimate British treat. These biscuits are perfect for Doctor Who-themed parties or just a fun snack while binge-watching your favorite episodes. So, go ahead and indulge in this delicious and easy-to-make treat.
