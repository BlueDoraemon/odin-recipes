Adipose Marshmallow Treats

Are you a fan of the Doctor Who episode "Partners in Crime" and those adorable little Adipose creatures? Well, now you can bring them to life in your kitchen with this fun and easy Adipose Marshmallow Treat recipe! These treats are perfect for any Doctor Who fan, and are sure to be a hit at your next viewing party or cosplay event. With just a few simple ingredients and a little bit of creativity, you can create your very own army of marshmallow Adipose that are almost too cute to eat. So grab your sonic screwdriver and let's get cooking!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup (1 stick) unsalted butter
  • 1 package (10 oz) mini marshmallows
  • 6 cups crispy rice cereal
  • 1 cup white chocolate chips
  • 1 tsp vegetable shortening
  • Black decorating gel

Method

  1. Melt the butter in a large pot over low heat.
  2. Add the mini marshmallows to the pot and stir until completely melted and well combined.
  3. Remove the pot from the heat and stir in the crispy rice cereal until it's evenly coated with the marshmallow mixture.
  4. Grease a 9x13 inch baking dish with non-stick cooking spray and pour the cereal mixture into the dish.
  5. Use a spatula or your hands (be careful, it might be hot!) to press the cereal mixture evenly into the dish.
  6. Let the mixture cool for about 30 minutes until it's firm and set.
  7. Use a cookie cutter or knife to cut out Adipose shapes from the cereal treats. Set aside.
  8. Melt the white chocolate chips and vegetable shortening in a microwave-safe bowl in 30-second intervals, stirring after each interval until completely melted and smooth.
  9. Dip each Adipose shape into the melted white chocolate and use a fork to gently shake off any excess chocolate.
  10. Place the Adipose on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper and let them cool and harden for a few minutes.
  11. Use the black decorating gel to add the Adipose's features, such as the eyes and mouth.
  12. Enjoy your Adipose Marshmallow Treats, and don't blink or you might miss them!
